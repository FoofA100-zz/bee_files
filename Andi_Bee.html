Andi Brady CPDT-KA, PMCT

Andi grew up living with and loving animals. This love was shown through the abundance of strays she brought home!

As an adult, Andi adopted a dog with serious behavioral issues which led her to study animal behavior. It began as a necessity but quickly became a passion. In the 7 years since then, Andi has studied with some of the leading trainers in the positive reinforcement training field. She is certified by the CCPDT as well as Pat Miller.

Andi believes that training should be fun for both human and dog alike! She can help with behavioral issues, basic manners, puppy training, and even help find the perfect dog to fit in your lifestyle.

Today Andi lives in Brooklyn, NY with her adopted pittie Rowan.
